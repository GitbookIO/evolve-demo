Smart routing v2: how we lifted approval rates 1–3%

By Lena Park, Staff Engineer, Payments — April 29, 2026

The dirty secret of card processing is that the same charge — same card, same amount, same merchant — gets approved by one acquirer and declined by another, all the time. Smart routing v1 picked an acquirer based on geography and currency. Smart routing v2 picks based on per-card-type historical approval rates, network token availability, and transaction shape — and falls back to a secondary acquirer on soft declines without the customer noticing.

Across the beta cohort we saw a 1–3% lift in approval rates, with the largest gains on subscription renewals and high-value B2B charges. There's nothing for customers to integrate — Smart routing is on by default for accounts where it's eligible. To customize routing rules or opt out, see Settings → Routing in the dashboard.
