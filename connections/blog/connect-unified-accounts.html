Killing Express, Standard, and Custom: the unified Connect account model

By Maria Chen, Engineering Manager, Connect — February 8, 2026

Anyone who's built a marketplace on the legacy Connect model knows the pain: you pick Express, Standard, or Custom up front, and the choice locks you into a feature set you may regret six months later. Custom gives you the most control but the most compliance liability; Express is fastest to launch but limits branding; Standard sits awkwardly between. Our customers asked, repeatedly, to mix and match.

The unified account model collapses all three into one object with composable feature flags — branding, dashboard access, liability, payout control. Existing Express/Standard/Custom accounts continue to work; new platforms get the unified model by default. The bulk-migration tool maps your existing accounts in a few minutes; we've run it across about 400 platforms so far without any compatibility breaks.
