Migrate from Stripe to Evolve in a weekend (live build)

Channel: Evolve · Duration: 28:31 · Published: February 4, 2026 · 41,392 views

An unedited migration from Stripe to Evolve, with all the things that actually went wrong on the first try. We cover field mapping, network token re-tokenization, mandate continuity, and the parallel-run pattern that lets you cut over without taking your production payments offline. By the end you'll have a checklist you can apply to your own migration.

Chapters

  • 2:18 — Field mapping: Customer, PaymentMethod, Subscription, Charge
  • 7:42 — Re-tokenization on first charge (the 2-day soft-decline bump)
  • 12:10 — Mandate continuity: don't trigger fresh SCA challenges
  • 16:55 — Parallel-run pattern: charge both sides, reconcile, swap
  • 22:08 — The cutover: webhook switch, DNS, and the rollback plan
  • 26:30 — Post-migration: what to monitor for the first 7 days

Transcript highlights

"…the parallel-run pattern is the safety net. You charge through both Stripe and Evolve for two billing cycles, reconcile that the totals match, and only then flip the source-of-truth. It costs you double processing fees for those cycles but you can roll back at any point in the first 60 days without losing data…"

"…network tokens don't transfer. We re-tokenize on first charge, and you'll see a small bump in soft declines for ~48 hours. By day 3 it's settled. Don't panic-roll-back unless declines exceed your normal envelope…"
